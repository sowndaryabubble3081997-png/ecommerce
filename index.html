APPLE

An apple is a sweet, fleshy, and round pome fruit that comes in various colors like red, green, and yellow, and is popular worldwide for its taste and nutritional value. It is a good source of fiber, vitamin C, and various antioxidants, which are linked to potential health benefits like improved heart health and weight management. Apples can be eaten fresh, used in cooking, or made into products like juice and sauce.

STRAWBERRY

A strawberry is a red, juicy, and sweet fruit known for its pleasant aroma and unique appearance, with its tiny seeds on the outside. It is a versatile fruit, consumed fresh or used in jams, desserts, and various culinary products.
